TV in Hospitality Mode?
Hotel TVs often lock smart features. Try these:

Ask the front desk for the TV's IP address or WiFi casting instructions — they usually know.

Check for a hotel casting app — many hotels use systems like Chromecast, Staycast, or Enseo. Look for instructions on the hotel welcome screen or on a card near the TV.

Use HDMI — if the TV has an HDMI port on the side, get a Lightning-to-HDMI adapter (Apple sells one, or try the hotel gift shop).
